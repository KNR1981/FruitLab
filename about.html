About Fruit Lab

The Science of Pure Vitality

Fruit Lab was created with one simple belief—nature already provides the perfect nutrition. Every bottle we produce is crafted using 100% cold-pressed technology, preserving the natural taste, vitamins, and nutrients of fresh fruits without compromising their goodness. Cold-pressing minimizes heat and oxidation, which is why it is widely used by premium juice brands to help retain freshness and flavor.

Unlike conventional beverages, our juices and smoothies contain no added sugar, no artificial additives, no preservatives, and no unnecessary ingredients. Every sip delivers the authentic taste of real fruit—just as nature intended.

We work directly with trusted local farmers, sourcing only fresh, carefully selected fruits at their peak quality. By building strong relationships with farming communities, we ensure every ingredient meets our standards for freshness, purity, and consistency.

At Fruit Lab, we are passionate about redefining healthy beverages in India. Our mission is to offer premium cold-pressed juices and smoothies that combine exceptional taste with uncompromising quality. Every bottle reflects our promise of honesty, freshness, and originality—because when it comes to nutrition, nothing beats real fruit.

Our Core Pillars

100% Cold-Pressed

We extract juice under pressure without producing heat or oxidation. This preserves 100% of the live enzymes, minerals, and vitamin content.

Biomedical Balance

Each flavor combination is carefully coordinated to boost target biometrics: strength, immunity, cellular hydration, and clean detoxification.

Zero Additives

No added sugar. No preservatives. No pasteurization. Only pure, raw nature in a premium glass bottle.

No Artificial Additives

Real fruits transformed into premium juices and smoothies with complete transparency. No preservatives, artificial colors, flavors, or chemicals.

Crafting the science of pure vitality. We serve 100% cold-pressed raw juices, active smoothies, and clean wellness bowls with zero sugar, zero additives, and maximum nutrients.
